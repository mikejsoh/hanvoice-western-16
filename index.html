About HanVoice


Main Branch

HanVoice was founded by a group of passionate law students from the Osgoode Hall Law School of York University. The group started as a grassroots support organization and developed into Canada’s largest nonpro t organization advocating for the improved human rights in North Korea. Hanvoice today, is engaged in both political and grassroots advocacy to raise awareness among both Canadian decision makers as well as the general Canadian public. The Hanvoice political advocacy campaign is “giving voice to the voiceless” and to pursue this vision, HanVoice has consulted with numerous members of parliament, ministers and of cials in addition to various other departments and agencies at the Federal and municipal level.


HanVoice at Western

Hanvoice is one of the four University chapters across Ontario. Our mission is to inform and empower the Western student body to mobilize each individual to affect the lives of North Koreans. The executive team currently consists of 27 passion-driven students seeking to work towards a better future for North Korea. The Western chapter is greatly expanding each year with both executive and general members. HanVoice Western hopes to further engage both the University community and London community in the discussions of North Korea and the surrounding issues.

  • Guest Speaker: Audrey's Campus Tour November 2016

    HanVoice is delighted to present another Guest Speaker Session with this year’s Pioneer, Audrey Park!

    Audrey is the third Pioneer of the HanVoice Pioneers Project. The objective of this project is to “have a North Korean resettled in South Korea begin to empower and build capacity within their own diaspora, and then take their efforts further to the broader North Korean Human Rights and Refugee Movement”.

    Audrey has escaped North Korea in 2006 with her mother. She is currently working on her Master’s in Politics at Yonsei University in South Korea. Through this program, Audrey had the opportunity to work with organizations such as KCWA and has recently started an internship at Parliament Hill under various Politicians.
